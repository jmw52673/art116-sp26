Commissions

At Jessys creation, we make sure every item is handmade with love!

How do I request a commission?

There is a form below this post where you can request a custom-made item!

How long does it take to get a custom made item?

A custom-made will take from 14 days to 3 months to complete, depending on the item.

Will my item be accepted as long as I put in a request?

Putting in a request for a custom item doesn't mean you will get your request accepted. All custom-made items will be approved or denied within reason. Things that are complicated and NSFW content won't be accepted.

How much will my custom made item cost

All custom-made items will have their own individual price depending on what is made and the cost of materials.

Do I have to pay for a fee before hand?

Yes, before making an item or getting a custom piece, you'll have to pay 10% of the item's value before the item gets made.
